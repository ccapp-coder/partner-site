TinkerTaps

Effective January 1, 2025

TinkerTaps is a sensory and hand-eye coordination app for babies and toddlers, developed and published by Courtright Collective LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We take the privacy of your family seriously — especially when it comes to young children.

Our Core Commitment

TinkerTaps does not collect personal information from children. The app is designed to be used by children under parental supervision and does not require account creation, login, or any personal data to function.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information from app users
  • We do not collect data on children's activity within the app
  • We do not serve behavioral advertising or track users across apps
  • We do not sell user data to third parties
  • There are no third-party advertising SDKs in TinkerTaps

Information We Do Collect

When you subscribe to TinkerTaps Premium, your subscription is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly handle or store your payment information — all billing is managed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.

If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (such as app version and crash reports) through Apple and Google's built-in developer tools. This data does not identify individual users and is used only to improve the app.

COPPA Compliance

TinkerTaps is designed for use by children under 13 with parental supervision. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require children to provide any personal information to use it.

Parents or guardians who believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13 should contact us immediately at the address below and we will delete that information promptly.

Parental Controls

TinkerTaps includes a built-in Parental Lock feature so parents can control access to settings and ensure children remain within the app experience.

The T-Shirt Feature

Subscribers can take a screenshot of their child's artwork and submit it to our Etsy shop for a custom t-shirt. This process happens entirely outside the app — we do not receive or process images through TinkerTaps itself. Any order placed through our Etsy shop is governed by Etsy's privacy policy.

Newsletter

If you sign up for our email newsletter through our website, we collect your email address to send you updates about TinkerTaps. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date and, where required by law, providing additional notice.

Trivd

Effective January 1, 2025

Trivd is a trivia app developed and published by Courtright Collective LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Trivd app or the Trivd website tools.

Information You Provide

Trivd is designed to work without requiring an account. When you play without signing in:

  • Your stats and streak data are stored locally on your device only
  • No personal information is sent to our servers
  • Game data is not linked to your identity in any way

If you choose to create an account to save your stats, we collect your email address and a display name. This data is stored securely and used only to provide the account feature.

Multiplayer and Room Codes

When you create or join a multiplayer game room, we temporarily store the room code, player display names chosen for that session, and game scores for the duration of the game. This data is deleted when the game session ends and is not linked to any persistent user profile unless you are signed in.

Daily Challenge (Website)

The Daily Trivia Challenge on our website uses your browser's local storage to track your daily streak. This data lives only on your device and is never sent to our servers. No personal information is collected to play the Daily Challenge.

Trivia Night Generator (Website)

When you use the Trivia Night Generator, your category selections and preferences are sent to our server to generate questions. We do not log or retain this data after the response is returned to you.

Analytics

We may use anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand how the app is being used — such as which topics are most popular or how many games are played per day. This data does not identify individual users and is used only to improve Trivd.

Advertising

The free version of Trivd includes advertisements served through third-party ad networks. These networks may collect certain device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies to serve relevant ads. You can remove all ads by upgrading to Trivd Pro.

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers.

Age Requirement

Trivd is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Trivd grows. We will update the effective date at the top of this page when changes are made.
