The Studio

How we work.

Courtright Collective is an app studio out of Nashville. Every app we build or partner on passes the same test: does it need to exist, and does it respect the person using it?

We take what we've learned building and shipping our own apps, and we put those resources to work on yours. Getting great ideas out of someone's head and into the hands of people who'll actually love them — that's the whole job.

"We only win when you win — that's the whole model."

If you've built something — or even just have the idea and need someone to help bring it to life — and need a partner who will treat it the same way you do, this is the conversation to have.

  • No startup fees for indie app partnerships — ever.
  • Revenue share model with transparent, sliding-scale splits.
  • Ad spend recoups before developer payouts begin.
  • Small portfolio by design — every app gets real attention.
  • Built by a founder who uses the apps, not just ships them.
  • App publishing, distribution, and growth strategy included.

What We Offer

Custom App Services.

App Already Built? We'll Ship It.

Got an app finished or near-finished and just need it in the stores? We handle the full App Store and Google Play submission — from setup to approval. Revenue share only, no upfront cost.

🛠️

Need It Built? We Can Do That Too.

Have a great idea but no developer? We build apps for a flat development fee. You own the product. We can partner on distribution afterward or hand it off entirely — your call.

📈

Growth Strategy

Launch plans, ASO, and growth frameworks built for indie-scale budgets. We've done it ourselves. We'll show you what actually works.

Revenue Share Model

For distribution partners — no upfront costs. We take a percentage of revenue we build together. We only win when you win. That keeps us honest in ways money alone can't.
